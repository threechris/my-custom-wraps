Shot Coach

Real-time feedback on your shooting form, straight from your phone's camera. Everything runs on-device — no video leaves your phone.

  1. Prop your phone up side-on, about 10–15 ft away, so your whole body (head to feet) and the ball's first arc are in frame.
  2. Shoot like normal. Each shot is detected automatically.
  3. Hear and see feedback after every rep — release angle, arc trace, legs, elbow, follow-through, guide hand.

Works best in good light with a standard orange ball and an uncluttered background. Landscape orientation gives the ball more room to fly.

Feedback appears here after each shot. Keep your whole body in frame; the skeleton overlay confirms tracking. The blue arm is the one being analyzed — switch shooting hand in settings if it's wrong.
